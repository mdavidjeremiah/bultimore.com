Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying, selling, and bidding on Artmart.

How does bidding work?

Register an account, browse available auctions, place your bid, and if you're the highest bidder when the auction closes, you win the artwork. We'll notify you immediately.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AmEx), bank transfers, PayPal, and select digital wallets. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How is artwork authenticated?

Every artwork undergoes a rigorous authentication process by our team of experts. We verify provenance, condition, and authenticity before listing.

What about shipping and insurance?

We handle professional packing, worldwide shipping, and full insurance for all artworks. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination.

Can I sell my artwork on Artmart?

Yes! Artists and collectors can submit artwork for consideration. Our curation team reviews all submissions for quality and market suitability.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy if the artwork doesn't match the description. Returns must be in original condition with all documentation.

Ready to Start Your Art Collection?

Join thousands of collectors who have discovered incredible artworks through Artmart. Whether you're a first-time buyer or an experienced collector, we're here to guide your journey.

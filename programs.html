Our Initiatives

Each of our programs is designed to address specific community needs while promoting sustainable development, education, and empowerment.

Dear Daughter

A movement to uplift, protect, and educate young girls around the world. Inspired by the voices of women, fathers, and mentors, this initiative challenges cultural limits and celebrates the power of education and dignity.

  • Empowering girls through education
  • Safe spaces for mentorship
  • Community workshops and resources
  • Building confidence and leadership
Learn More

Ingram's Push for Reading

A literary and educational initiative dedicated to promoting the habit of reading among young people and adults alike. We believe that reading transforms lives, sharpens minds, and strengthens societies.

  • Promoting literacy across communities
  • Book drives and digital access
  • Mentorship programs
  • Community reading hubs

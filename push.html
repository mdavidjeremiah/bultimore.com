Our Mission

Ingram's Push is a literary and educational initiative dedicated to promoting the habit of reading among young people and adults alike. We believe that reading transforms lives, sharpens minds, and strengthens societies. Our goal is to foster curiosity, literacy, and lifelong learning across communities through mentorship programs, book drives, and digital access.
